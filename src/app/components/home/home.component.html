Welcome to Shoutouts for Streamers

Shoutouts for Streamers will keep the streamers shoutouts alive on their Twitch page for all viewers to see. There is nothing extra to do to get the extension functional on a channel, just install and continue to use your current shoutout system. Only the broadcaster and channel mods can add shoutouts to the list.
