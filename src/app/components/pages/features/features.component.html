Features

Make Shoutouts for Streamers fit your branding. Modify the extension typography. Change the colors of the font, borders and background.

Shoutouts for Streamers has two built-in shoutout commands. If your community uses another command to give a shoutout, we got you covered. Add more commands to trigger Shoutouts for Streamers.

No configuration required. Just install Shoutouts for Streamers from your Twitch dashboard and that's it. You can make modifications but you don't need to do a thing after the installation, it just works.
